FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Everything you might want to know about running your team on OTeams. Still curious? Get started or explore the Roxan intelligence layer.

OTeams is a new way for your whole company to work. Instead of email, it's faster, better-organized and more secure — everything is organized into channels that are easy to create, join and search. It brings Roxan into the flow of work, so it's faster to find and act on what you need, and it connects to the rest of your stack to become one operating system for all your work.
Work in OTeams starts with channels. Create one for each project, team or office and you give every conversation a home. Because channels are easy to join, OTeams adapts as things change — add someone new and they can scroll up to catch up, or spin up a fresh channel when something new begins.
Roxan runs on OTeams' trusted infrastructure and upholds the same security and compliance standards as OTeams itself. We don't share your data with model providers, and we don't use your data to train foundation models — the models are hosted within Oplo's own private cloud, so your data stays in-house.
Yes — you can securely discuss confidential information in OTeams. It delivers enterprise-grade security at every layer and is built to meet standards including SOC 2, ISO 27001 and GDPR, and can be configured for HIPAA. Advanced controls like Enterprise Key Management give admins fine-grained control over encryption, and you can plug in your own security tools for instant threat alerts.
Yes. Unlike email, OTeams isn't exposed to spam or phishing — the cause of most data breaches. Your handle can't be sold to advertisers, and you only ever hear from people inside your organization or trusted partners using OTeams Connect. Granular admin controls tailor security per user, so no one sees what they shouldn't.
OTeams Connect is a more secure, productive way for organizations to work together. It moves conversations with your partners, clients and vendors into OTeams, replacing external email. Enterprise-grade security and compliance — including Enterprise Key Management — extend to every Connect conversation.
