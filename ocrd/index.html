Our work

We do research in the open, and share it with the world.

01 — Open

Published, not locked away

Papers, code, and datasets released for anyone to read, reproduce, and build on. Progress compounds when it's shared.

02 — Rigorous

Evidence over intuition

We hold our results to the standards of the fields we work in, and report what we find — including when it surprises us.

03 — Consequential

Weighed against impact

Every advance is measured by its effect on real people. Safety, interpretability, and society sit at the center, not the edge.

Featured finding

Capability is compounding.

Society & Economics

Frontier model capability has grown roughly 10× per year.

Tracking a fixed suite of reasoning, coding, and science benchmarks since 2021, aggregate capability rises about an order of magnitude annually — a straight line on a log scale. Understanding what bends that line, up or down, is central to OCRD's work.

Source: OCRD Capability Index, 2021–2026 · n = 40 benchmarks
