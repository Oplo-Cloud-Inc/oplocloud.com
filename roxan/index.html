For developers

Build on Roxan.

One SDK, every model. Call Roxan from anything with a few lines — streaming, tools, vision, and voice included. Runs in the Oplo cloud or on-device.

Get an API key → Read the docs 
# pip install oplo
from oplo import Roxan

roxan = Roxan(model="roxan-flash")

reply = roxan.chat(
    "Summarize this quarter and draft the email.",
    tools=[odocs, osheets, omails],
)
print(reply.text)

Safety & privacy

Powerful, and responsible.

Intelligence you can trust with your life's work. The everyday model runs on your own device, and nothing trains on your data without your say.

  • ✓ On-device by default — Roxan Nano runs on your hardware, not ours.
  • ✓ Your data is never used to train models unless you opt in.
  • ✓ Every action an agent takes is logged, reversible, and yours to approve.
  • ✓ Independent safety evaluations before every release.
